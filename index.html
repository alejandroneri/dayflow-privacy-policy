Privacy Policy – Dayflow

Last updated: March 2026

Dayflow respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the Dayflow mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Dayflow does not require account registration.

User-Provided Data

  • Financial information such as accounts, expenses, incomes, balances, and habits entered by the user.
  • This data is stored locally on the user’s device and is not uploaded to external servers.

Advertising Data

  • Dayflow uses Google AdMob to display advertisements.
  • AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data according to Google’s Privacy Policy.

In-App Purchases

  • Dayflow offers optional in-app purchases.
  • Payments are processed securely by Google Play Billing.
  • Dayflow does not store or process payment information.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide and maintain app functionality
  • Improve user experience
  • Display advertisements
  • Enable premium features

3. Data Sharing

Dayflow does not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties.

Third-party services used:

  • Google AdMob (advertising)
  • Google Play Billing (payments)

4. Data Storage and Security

  • All user data is stored locally on the device.
  • Reasonable security measures are applied.
  • No personal financial data is transmitted to remote servers.

5. Children’s Privacy

Dayflow is intended for users 18 years of age and older. The app does not knowingly collect data from children. Access by minors is restricted through Google Play.

6. User Rights

  • Users may delete all data by uninstalling the app.
  • Ad personalization can be managed through Google Ads settings.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
